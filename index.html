Limitless Hope Kids

We are Limitless Hope Kids, a non-profit led by highschoolers who strongly care about leveling the playing field for kids experiencing disabilities. Our goal is to bring together kids and families to spread awareness and to fundraise through activities. All gifts will be donated to organizations that work with those kids. We hope you can join us in our journey!

Meet the Team

Meet the passionate highschoolers behind Limitless Hope Kids. We are dedicated to making a difference in the lives of kids experiencing disabilities and their families.

Sherry Chen

CO-FOUNDER

Hi! I'm Sherry, founder of Limitless Hope Kids. Inspired by a family member with neurodivergence, I'm passionate about supporting children with cognitive disabilities and interested in data science and art!

Paige Wanniappa

CO-FOUNDER

Hi! I'm Paige, co-founder of Limitless Hope Kids. Inspired by my younger cousins, I'm passionate about ensuring all kids can experience joy, and Im interested in finance and accounting, with a love for movies—especially The Lorax.
